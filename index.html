QueerValues

What is QueerValues?

QueerValues is a political compass test based on the SapplyValues test* with the UI of 8values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.

There are questions in the test.

What does this test do exactly?

We assume in this test that you have a basic sense of your own sexuality. The test is not about attraction to specific genders, but rather the amount of genders and your stance on gender alltogether.

What are the axes?

The Romantic axis (Panromantic-Aromantic) tests your sense of romantic attraction. This entails your ability to have a relationship outside of sex. Panromantic people can form romantic bonds with any gender, aromantic people cannot form romantic bonds at all.
The Sexual axis (Pansexual-Asexual) tests your sense of sexual attraction. Pansexuals can have sexual relations with any gender, asexuals do not feel sexual attraction at all.
The Progressive axis (Progressivist-Traditionalist) tests your opinion on modern LGBTQ+ values, including the sense of multiple genders, non-binaryism, and anything else that strays beyond the Gay-Straight-Bi standard. Progressivists embrace these modern values, while Traditionalists would rather keep it as it always has been.
